How we work

The PBI approach

Three principles that govern every engagement.

01
Independence is non-negotiable
PBI has no vendor relationships and no products to sell. The only financial interest in any engagement is delivering the right answer — not the answer that protects a partnership or promotes a tool. This is the foundation everything else is built on.
02
Technical depth, not surface advice
AI advisory that can't evaluate model behavior, benchmark claims, or regulatory exposure isn't advisory — it's repackaged vendor messaging. PBI brings original research experience to every client conversation, including published work on LLM robustness and failure modes.
03
Decisions, not deliverables
Every engagement is scoped around the decision you're facing — not a standard framework applied to your organization. The output is always actionable: something your team can fund, stop, or build. Reports that sit in a folder are not the goal.
